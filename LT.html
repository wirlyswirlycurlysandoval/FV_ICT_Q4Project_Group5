LT!


Leadership Training (LT) is a subject that helps students develop the skills and values needed to guide others responsibly. In the Philippine context, LT often includes learning about laws, rules, and civic duties, which prepare students to become disciplined and law-abiding citizens. It teaches the importance of respect, fairness, and accountability in both personal and community life.

In Grade 9, LT encourages students to practice teamwork, communication, and decision-making. These skills are essential not only for leading groups but also for understanding how leadership connects to responsibility under the law. By studying LT, students learn that true leadership is not about power, but about service, integrity, and setting a good example for others.

This subject is valuable because it prepares young people to become responsible leaders who can contribute positively to society and uphold the values of the nation.
