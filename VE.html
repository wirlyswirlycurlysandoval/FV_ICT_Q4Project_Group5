VE!


Values Education (VE) is a subject that focuses on building character and guiding students to make good choices in life. It teaches important values such as respect, honesty, responsibility, and compassion, which help us grow into better individuals. In Grade 9, VE encourages students to reflect on their actions and understand how values shape relationships at home, in school, and in the community.


Through lessons and activities, VE helps us practice discipline, empathy, and cooperation. These values are not only useful in academics but also in everyday life, as they guide us in becoming responsible citizens. By studying VE, students learn that success is not measured only by knowledge or skills, but also by the strength of one’s character.
