PE!


Physical Education (PE) is a subject that focuses on health, fitness, and the importance of an active lifestyle. It teaches students how exercise strengthens the body, improves coordination, and builds endurance. In Grade 9, PE often includes activities such as sports, dance, and fitness training, which encourage teamwork and discipline.


Beyond physical skills, PE also promotes values like cooperation, respect, and fair play. It reminds us that staying active is not only good for our bodies but also for our mental well-being. By participating in PE, students learn habits that can lead to a healthier and more balanced life, both in school and beyond.
