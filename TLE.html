TLE!


Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE) is a subject that teaches practical skills we can use in everyday life. One important area of TLE is cooking, which involves not only preparing food but also learning proper kitchen and table manners. These lessons help students understand the importance of cleanliness, safety, and respect when handling food.

In Grade 9, TLE also emphasizes how to properly plate and serve meals. Good plating makes food look more appealing, while proper serving shows courtesy to others. By practicing these skills, students learn discipline, creativity, and responsibility.

TLE is valuable because it prepares us for real-life situations, whether at home or in future careers. It teaches us that cooking is not just about taste—it is also about presentation, safety, and respect for those we serve.
