ICT!


Information and Communications Technology (ICT) is a subject that teaches students how to use computers and digital tools effectively. One important part of ICT is coding, which allows us to create programs, websites, and applications. In Grade 9, learning coding helps students understand how technology works and how it can be used to solve problems creatively.


ICT also develops skills in logic, problem-solving, and innovation. By writing code, students learn to think step by step and pay attention to detail. These skills are valuable not only in school but also in future careers, since technology plays a major role in almost every field today. Studying ICT prepares students to be confident and capable in a digital world.
