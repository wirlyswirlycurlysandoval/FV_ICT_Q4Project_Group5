Filipino!


Filipino is a subject that strengthens our identity and culture as Filipinos. It teaches us how to use our national language effectively in speaking, reading, and writing. In Grade 9, Filipino lessons often include grammar, literature, and communication skills, which help students express themselves clearly and appreciate the richness of our language.


Through stories, poems, and essays, Filipino also connects us to our history and traditions. It encourages pride in our heritage while promoting respect for others. By studying Filipino, students not only improve their language skills but also deepen their understanding of what it means to be part of the Filipino community.
